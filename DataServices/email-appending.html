Email Appending Services

Enhance your database with permission-based email addresses to boost your multi-channel marketing strategies.

Our Email Appending Process

Your file is matched against our permission-based email databases, ensuring the email address corresponds to the contact listed in your database, not a random employee.

We also offer a domain pattern matching process, identifying the company’s email domain and naming convention (e.g., firstname.lastname@ or firstname@) using reference databases to generate accurate email addresses.

Once processing is complete, a welcome email is sent to your business customers, introducing your online communications and offering an opt-out option.

After the program, we return the enhanced file, ready for you to leverage in multi-channel marketing strategies.
