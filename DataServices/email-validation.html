Email Validation Service

Reduce bounce rates and ensure the accuracy of your email database with our advanced email validation and verification solutions.

Our 5-Step Email Validation Process

Our comprehensive email validation process ensures your database is clean and accurate, minimizing bounce rates.

  • 1

    Keyword Scrub

    We remove problematic words like "spam," "junk," and "abuse" from your email list to ensure quality.

  • 2

    Bounce File Compare

    We compare your list against our 19.5 million bounce records, removing emails that have bounced, sent challenge responses, or requested removal.

  • 3

    Complainers File

    We remove matches from our complainers file, including 75% of SpamCop database members, to avoid problematic contacts.

  • 4

    Domain Validation

    We eliminate spam seeds, bots, blacklisted, or bogus domains from your list, using our database of over 156,000 domains.

  • 5

    Email Verification

    Our proprietary software validates each email address to confirm it can accept emails, ensuring deliverability.

Why Choose Our Email Validation?

With a 98% accuracy guarantee, our service ensures your email campaigns reach the right audience.

You only pay for email addresses that are successfully validated, deliverable, and remain opted-in after receiving an opt-out message.
